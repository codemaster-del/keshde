# Privacy Policy for Kesde

**Effective date:** June 23, 2026

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to **Kesde** ("we", "our", or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how the Kesde mobile application (the "App") handles information when you use it.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. Please read this policy carefully.

## 2. Information We Collect

**Kesde does not collect, store, or share any personal information.**

The App does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal details. No data is transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

All content you save (such as liked fashion styles) is stored **locally on your device only** and never leaves it.

## 3. How the App Works

Kesde is a free fashion inspiration app for women. You can:

- Browse women's clothing styles and designs
- Like styles you enjoy
- Save liked styles locally on your device

No internet connection is required to use saved content, and no usage data is tracked or collected.

## 4. Data Storage

Any data generated by your use of the App (such as your liked items) is stored exclusively on your device using local storage. We have no access to this data. If you uninstall the App, this data will be permanently deleted from your device.

## 5. Third-Party Services

Kesde does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not use Google Analytics, Firebase, Facebook SDK, or any similar services.

## 6. Children's Privacy

Kesde does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The App is safe for all ages.

## 7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Effective date" at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
